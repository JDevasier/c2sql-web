Political Claims Fact-Checker

About the Project

Our research project focuses on analyzing and verifying claims made by political figures. In an era of information overload, it's crucial to separate truth from misinformation. We aim to provide accurate and unbiased information to the public.

Fact-Checking with SQL

Our process involves harnessing the power of SQL to efficiently manage and query vast amounts of data. We design sophisticated SQL queries to sift through historical records enabling us to verify the accuracy of claims.

Frames Model

We utilize advanced frames models, which enable us to contextualize statements and claims within broader narratives. These models allow us to assess not only the factual accuracy of claims but also their implications within political discourse and historical contexts, providing a comprehensive analysis.

Data Collection

Collecting accurate and reliable data is a cornerstone of our project. We source information from diverse and reputable datasets, ensuring our fact-checking process is based on credible evidence.
